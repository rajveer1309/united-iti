Get Started

United ITI provides skill training courses that can be enrolled in after completing class 10th or 12th.

ITI courses have traditionally been quite popular among students, particularly those from rural areas, because they offer courses that focus on skill development. Students who graduate from ITIs are skilled professionals, either in the engineering or non-engineering trades.

Our Mission :

Develop technicians and trained labour in various vocational skills in response to industrial demand, allowing them to find industrial jobs.

Improving individuals' employability and adaptability to new technologies and labour market demands.

Improving people's productivity and living conditions.

Increasing the state's competitiveness.

Obtaining investment for skill development.

Why Choose iti?

ITI is an excellent education for students who desire to pursue a professional technical course in a short period of time.

  • Many Govt. and Private jobs are Available according to their stream.
  • It enables you to start your own business and work for yourself.
  • There are numerous career prospects, particularly for specialist crafts such as Fritters, with worldwide oil and gas plants, shipyards, and so on.
